# Goldman Sachs

> Goldman Sachs is a leading global money-center investment bank whose public developer surface is institutional and professional-client oriented. It spans the Marquee digital platform (content, pricing & risk, hedging, indices, portfolio and market-data services plus the open-source GS Quant Python toolkit), the Transaction Banking (TxB) embedded-banking APIs for virtual accounts, payments and reporting across Fedwire/ACH/SWIFT rails, and the RIA Custody (GS Custody Solutions) REST APIs for advisor firms. Access is gated to institutional clients and approved partners; there is no self-serve consumer open-finance API.

## APIs

- [Content Services API](https://developer.gs.com/docs/): institutional Goldman Sachs content and commentary across equity, fixed income, currency and commodities markets (Marquee, OAuth 2.0).
- [Pricing & Risk Services API](https://developer.gs.com/docs/gsquant/pricing-and-risk/): derivatives pricing and risk analytics, the engine behind GS Quant (Marquee, OAuth 2.0).
- [Hedging Services API](https://developer.gs.com/docs/): portfolio hedging and optimization tools (Marquee, OAuth 2.0).
- [Index Services API](https://marquee.gs.com/welcome/our-platform/systematic-trading-strategies): indices, systematic strategies and bespoke basket construction (Marquee, OAuth 2.0).
- [Portfolio Services API](https://developer.gs.com/docs/): portfolio lifecycle management and reporting workflow (Marquee, OAuth 2.0).
- [Data Services API](https://marquee.gs.com/welcome/our-platform/data-services): 400+ datasets across Equities, Fixed Income, Currencies, Commodities and Digital Assets plus a Security Master (Marquee, OAuth 2.0).
- [Transaction Banking (TxB) API](https://developer.gs.com/docs/services/transaction-banking/): virtual accounts, payments/collections and reporting across Fedwire/ACH/SWIFT; mTLS + RSA-JWT step-up auth; documents webhooks.
- [RIA Custody API](https://developer.gs.com/docs/services/ria-custody): custody REST APIs for advisor firms — onboarding, data files, ACH/wire transactions (OAuth 2.0).

## SDKs

- [GS Quant (Python)](https://pypi.org/project/gs-quant/): official open-source (Apache-2.0) Python toolkit wrapping the Marquee pricing, risk and data APIs — `pip install gs-quant`. Source: https://github.com/goldmansachs/gs-quant
